About Me

Hello! I'm Manoush Fatima, a student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (BSCS) at IU International University of Applied Sciences. While my academic journey is deeply rooted in technology, my heart beats for art. I've always had a passion for creativity, and I see the world through an artistic lens. My inspiration comes from the most unexpected places — from the variant patterns of nature, the vibrant colors , to the quiet emotions in everyday life. Every experience sparks a new idea for a piece of art.

Welcome to my portfolio — a journey through my imagination and a blend of technology and creativity, as I strive to bring both my worlds together.

ART GALLERY

"African Women"

A vibrant celebration of strength and tradition, this painting depicts an "African Woman" holding a makta, her presence commanding the canvas. Bursting with bold colors, it captures culture, empowerment, and beauty in every stroke.

"Abstract Galaxy"

An explosion of color and energy, where black, blue, red, and purple drip and swirl in a cosmic dance. Though abstract, it evokes the mysterious beauty of the galaxy—capturing the intensity of the unknown and the allure of the universe.

"Kun Faya Kun"

Embracing the simplicity of ‘Kun Faya Kun’ — ‘Be, and it is’ — this minimalist painting that embodies divine creation. The design reflects the boundless power and will of the Almighty, evoking pure spiritual energy.

"Face of Reflection"

A captivating figure, where patterns meet . This painting invites the viewer into a world of rhythm and movement, creating a dynamic interplay between structure and freedom. A visual journey of endless possibilities.

"Floural Beauty"

An enchanting mix of vibrant blooms . Each flower with its own beauty, coming together in perfect harmony. The stunning contrasts make this piece truly captivating and stunning celebrating nature’s vibrant energy.

"Allahu Akbar"

This painting beautifully captures the essence of Islamic faith, the Kaaba, the Green Dome of Masjid Nabawi, and the Dome of the Rock, crowned by the words ‘Allahu Akbar’ tribute to devotion and unity.

SUFISM

The mystical journey of Sufism is symbolic of a soul's longing for divine love, which is reflected in poetry, music, and art. The concept of spiritual surrender, devotion, and transcendence are central themes in Sufism, and they are often represented in Sufi art through intricate symbols, geometrical designs, and abstract forms that express the ineffable. The piece encapsulates the idea that, through devotion and spiritual awakening, one may touch the infinite—a central theme in the practice and philosophy of Sufism.

ALLAH

This painting beautifully depicts the sacred name of Allah, written in a humble way that evokes a sense of devotion. The simplicity of the calligraphy contrasts with the vibrant, multi-colored background, symbolizing the infinite and diverse expressions of divine presence in the world. Each color blends seamlessly into the next, representing the richness of creation and the boundless nature of God's mercy. The profound depth of Allah’s name, offering beauty, spirituality, and color come together in perfect harmony.

MY INSPIRATION

Culture

As a Punjabi, I am deeply inspired by my vibrant culture. The rich traditions, festivals, and the colorful attire fill me with joy and creativity. It’s a constant source of inspiration in my work, reminding me to embrace diversity and color in everything I create.

Nature

Nature is another great inspiration for me. From the changing seasons to the beauty of the landscapes, nature’s colors, textures, and patterns help me find peace and creative flow. It teaches me balance and harmony, and I try to incorporate these elements into my work.

"Admiration"

Another major influence on my art is the work of other artists. Observing their techniques, creativity, and unique perspectives pushes me to explore new ideas and improve my craft. It's a reminder that there’s always something to learn from others in the artistic community.
